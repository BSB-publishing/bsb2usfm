About USFM Files
What are USFM files?

USFM (Unified Standard Format Markers) is a standard for encoding biblical text with formatting and structural information. These files can be used with various Bible translation and publishing tools.

Compatible Software:
  • Paratext
  • Translationcore
  • Translation Studio
  • Other USFM-compatible tools
